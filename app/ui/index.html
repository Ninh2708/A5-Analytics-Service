Trạng thái

Dùng phần biểu đồ bên dưới để xem xu hướng 7 ngày và bảng endpoint để kiểm tra sức khỏe dịch vụ.

POST /api/v1/events/access

Access Gate nhan swipe events, xu ly grant/deny va tra ve ket qua chuan hoa.

GET /api/v1/logs

Truy van logs gan nhat, phuc vu audit va debug RFID/UID.

GET /api/v1/analytics

Thong ke tong quan cua Access Gate: tong event, granted/denied, theo huong va theo cua.

POST /api/v1/analytics/sensor

Stores processed IoT sensor events with status, alert level and reason.

GET /api/v1/analytics/sensor

Lists stored sensor events with filters for device, status and alert level.

POST /api/v1/analytics/camera

Stores camera detections, motion score and risk level from A2 stream.

GET /api/v1/analytics/camera

Filters stored camera events by camera, risk and pagination.

POST /api/v1/analytics/vision-detections

Receives AI Vision detections for trend analysis and future dashboards.

Core proxy endpoints

Health, events, anomaly feed, alerts and summary are proxied to Core Business.

POST /notifications

Core Business gui alert sang Notification Service qua Telegram va Email.

GET /notifications

Analytics hoac dashboard co the tu dong cap nhat lich su thong ke cac alert da gui.
